Raw Unicode CLDR v48 date data, vendored verbatim.

Source: https://github.com/unicode-org/cldr-json  (tag 48.0.0)
        cldr-json/cldr-dates-full/main/<locale>/ca-gregorian.json

These are the official Unicode CLDR `cldr-dates-full` Gregorian-calendar files,
committed unmodified. `codegen` (emit_dates) reads them to produce
src/cldr/calendar.bin and src/cldr/skeletons.bin. Do not hand-edit; to refresh,
re-download from the same upstream path at the pinned CLDR version.
